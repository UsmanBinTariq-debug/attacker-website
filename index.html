üõ°Ô∏è Defender's Countermeasures

  • 1. Security Awareness Training Conduct regular phishing simulation exercises and train all employees to recognize social engineering attacks. Implement mandatory cybersecurity training quarterly with focus on email threats.
  • 2. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Enforce MFA on all accounts, especially for privileged users and remote access. Even if credentials are stolen, attackers cannot access systems without the second factor.
  • 3. Backup & Recovery Strategy Maintain offline, immutable backups using the 3-2-1 rule (3 copies, 2 media types, 1 offsite). Test restoration procedures monthly to ensure rapid recovery without paying ransom.
  • 4. Network Segmentation Segment critical systems and patient data networks. Implement zero-trust architecture to prevent lateral movement. Use VLANs and firewalls to isolate sensitive systems.
  • 5. Patch Management & EDR Deploy automated patch management for all systems. Implement Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) solutions with behavioral analysis to detect ransomware before encryption occurs. Monitor for suspicious file access patterns and privilege escalation attempts.
